About EduTutor Kenya

Connecting students with quality home tutors across Kenya since 2024

Our Mission

To make quality education accessible to every student in Kenya by connecting them with verified, qualified tutors.

EduTutor Kenya was founded with a simple goal: to bridge the gap between students seeking academic help and qualified tutors looking for teaching opportunities. We believe that personalized, one-on-one tutoring can transform a student's academic journey.

Whether you're struggling with Mathematics in Form 2, preparing for KCSE exams, or need help with university-level courses, we have the right tutor for you.
